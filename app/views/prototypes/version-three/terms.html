DfE Internal data sharing

You must agree to:

  1. Inform the data owner when you no longer require access to data.

  2. Only request access to data that is required for the purpose of your role or project.

  3. Do not download copies, share or merge data extracted from core systems without the permission of the data owner.

  4. Only keep data in an approved storage area or on your DfE encrypted device connected to the DfE domain.

  5. Report any requests for access to data outside of the formally agreed process.

  6. Only use, store, and process the data for the intended purpose and in the manner agreed with and approved by the data owner.

  7. Be familiar with the Data Retention periods set out for the datasets you access, use, store and process. This includes understanding any archive and deletion activity required at the end of an agreed retention period.

  8. Report any suspected or actual data breach or security incident immediately by raising the relevant Service Now form.

  9. Delete any data when requested to by the data owner - immediately.
