DfE Data Access and Control

Data user rules

You must agree to:

  1. Inform the data owner when you no longer require access to data.

  2. Request access to data that is required for the purpose of your role or project.

  3. Not download copies, share or merge data extracted from core systems without permission from the data owner.

  4. Only retain data in an approved storage area with relevant retention labels applied.

  5. Report any requests for access to data outside of the formally agreed process.

  6. Use, store, and process the data for the intended purpose and in the manner agreed with and approved by the data owner or data supply agreement.

  7. Store data in a secure approved location or tool, on your DfE encrypted device connected to the DfE domain.

  8. Be familiar with the Data Retention periods set out for the datasets you access, use, store and process. This includes understanding any archive and deletion activity required at the end of an agreed retention period.

  9. Report any suspected or actual data breach or security incident immediately by raising the relevant Service Now form.
