Tell us about your further education staff

Collecting accurate further education workforce data will help us to:

  • bid for more investment in further education
  • understand how our policy reforms impact staff (for example, introducing T-Levels)
  • understand pay and how it impacts recruitment and retention
  • identify skill gaps

This will help further education providers by:

  • allowing them to benchmark against other providers
  • giving them information to use for planning
  • giving further education workforce the same status as schools and higher education
  • reducing the number of workforce data requests they need to respond to

The time it takes to submit your data will depend on how many staff you employ.
